04 — FAQ
04 / FAQ

Questions.

How do I place an order?

DM me on Discord or use the contact form. Share what you need and a reference if you have one — we agree on scope and price before I start.

Why reference-based?

References align us on style before any work starts. Less back-and-forth, better result. A screenshot or a game you like is enough.

Do you accept TL?

Yes — TL is available on request. Robux is primary, but we can work out a TL rate per project.

How long does it take?

Simple frames: 1–2 days. Larger projects take longer. I'll give you a timeline estimate when we discuss the scope.

Will it work in Roblox Studio?

Yes. Everything is built with Studio constraints in mind — resolution, image upload sizes and available fonts all considered.
